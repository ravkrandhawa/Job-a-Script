About Us

Welcome to Job-a-Script, your personalized career companion in the tech industry. In a world where finding the right job can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack, we aim to simplify the process and help tackle unemployment in tech. With Job-a-Script, you complete a quick, insightful questionnaire, and our system matches your unique skills and preferences to job postings tailored just for you. Each job comes with a match score out of 10, giving you a clear indication of how well it aligns with your profile. The higher the score, the more customized the opportunity is to your needs and aspirations. Our mission is to empower tech talent with the right opportunities, bridging the gap between skilled professionals and their dream jobs.
